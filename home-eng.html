Get all the assistance you need getting set up and settled in your home in Taiwan

There's enough to worry about when leaving home to teach abroad, so we do our best to make it as smooth as possible. From the moment you apply to the moment you start you're very first class, we'll walk you through every step of the way. Enjoy a healthy work-life balance with a schedule that includes plenty of 'me time'.

Teaching abroad is just as much about travel and exploring as it is about teaching, so we don't expect you to always be on the grind. At Shane you can enjoy a regular work schedule with plenty of free time to explore, study Mandarin, work on personal projects, or just get some good old fashioned R&R. Our salary guarantee system also allows you to set your budget and make sure you save each month.

A quick glance at your students and classes

Shane caters mostly to students aged between 3-14 years old. Your schedule will consist mainly of group classes and possibly a few one-to-one classes depending on the school you're at. Our tailored curriculum includes everything you need to create fun and engaging classes every time. From teaching guides, to resources, to materials, we provide everything you need so you can focus on the most important part - delivering a great class that student enjoy and remember!
