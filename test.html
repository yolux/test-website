Salary and Benefits

Teaching at shane / Salary and Benefits


Earn a competitive starting salary

Shane English School Taiwan offers a competitive starting salary for first-time teachers and higher starting salaries for teachers with classroom experience. New teachers with a TEFL certificate of 100 hours (or more) will be started on NT$600/hour, while teachers with classroom experience can earn up to NT$640/ hour as a starting salary.

Career advancement opportunities

Teachers looking to stay in Taiwan for a longer duration are encouraged to pursue roles in Head Office. Positions in academia, course creation, publishing, recruitment and marketing often become available and will be advertised internally to give teachers the opportunity to move up within the organisation and remain part of the team at Shane English School Taiwan.

Our senior staff will be more than happy to share their stories.

During the recruitment process and teacher training week you'll have the opportunity to speak with a number of our staff, most of which began as teachers who started their career with Shane. If you're interested in career advancement opportunities ask them about their journey with Shane English School Taiwan. They'll be more than happy to share how they began, what they loved most about the job, and why they decided to stay with the company long term.
